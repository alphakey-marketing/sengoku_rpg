Core stats: STR, AGI, VIT, INT, DEX, LUK (1–99). They feed into ATK, MATK, DEF, MDEF, HIT, FLEE, CRIT, ASPD, Max HP, Max SP.
​

STR – Strength (Physical Power)
Design meaning: Increases melee damage and carry weight.

Melee weapon equipped:
StatusATK_STR = STR
WeaponATK_bonus%_from_STR = 0.5% × STR (applied only to base weapon ATK, optional).

Ranged weapon equipped:
StatusATK_STR = floor(STR / 5)

Weight limit:
WeightLimit = BaseWeight + 30 × STR

AGI – Agility (Speed & Dodge)
Design meaning: Increases attack speed and dodge.

FLEE_A (basic dodge):
FLEE_A = 100 + BaseLv + AGI + floor(LUK / 5)

Total FLEE shown:
FLEE = FLEE_A + PerfectDodge

SoftDEF from AGI:
SoftDEF_from_AGI = floor(AGI / 5)

ASPD (conceptual):
ASPD = ASPD_Base(class, weapon) + f(AGI, DEX)
(capped at 190 for pre‑3rd style).

VIT – Vitality (HP & Toughness)
Design meaning: Increases HP, item healing, and resistance.

Max HP:
MaxHP = ClassBaseHP(BaseLv) × (1 + 0.01 × VIT) + gearHP

SoftDEF from VIT:
SoftDEF_from_VIT = floor(VIT / 2)

SoftMDEF from VIT:
SoftMDEF_from_VIT = floor(VIT / 5)

HP regen bonus:
HPRegen_bonus = floor(VIT / 5)

Healing item multiplier:
HealItemHP_multiplier = 1 + 0.02 × VIT

INT – Intelligence (Magic & SP)
Design meaning: Increases magic damage, SP, and magic defense.

Status MATK:
StatusMATK_INT = 1.5 × INT

SoftMDEF from INT:
SoftMDEF_INT = INT

Max SP:
MaxSP = ClassBaseSP(BaseLv) × (1 + 0.01 × INT) + gearSP

SP regen bonus:
SPRegen_bonus = floor(INT / 6)

SP item multiplier:
HealItemSP_multiplier = 1 + 0.01 × INT

Variable cast time (simplified RO‑like):
VarCastFactor = 1 − (DEX / 150) − (INT / 300)
VarCastTime = VarCastBase × max(0.1, VarCastFactor)

DEX – Dexterity (Accuracy & Ranged)
Design meaning: Increases hit rate, cast speed, and ranged damage.

With ranged weapon:
StatusATK_DEX_ranged = DEX
WeaponATK_bonus%_from_DEX = 0.5% × DEX

With melee weapon:
StatusATK_DEX_melee = floor(DEX / 5)

HIT (accuracy):
HIT = 175 + BaseLv + DEX + floor(LUK / 3)

MATK from DEX (optional):
MATK_from_DEX = floor(DEX / 5)

SoftMDEF from DEX (optional):
SoftMDEF_from_DEX = floor(DEX / 5)

ASPD: DEX adds a small bonus together with AGI.

LUK – Luck (Crit & Misc)
Design meaning: Adds crits, perfect dodge, and small global bonuses.

Crit rate:
CRIT_rate = 0.3 × LUK + CRIT_from_gear

Perfect dodge:
PerfectDodge = floor(LUK / 10)

FLEE bonus from LUK:
FLEE_bonus_LUK = floor(LUK / 5)

ATK, MATK, HIT from LUK (RO‑like):
ATK_from_LUK = floor(LUK / 3)
MATK_from_LUK = floor(LUK / 3)
HIT_from_LUK = floor(LUK / 3)

Derived Stat Summary (recommended for your system)

StatusATK (physical):
StatusATK = STR + StatusATK_from_DEX + ATK_from_LUK

Total ATK as “A + B”:
A (Status ATK) = from STR/DEX/LUK.
B (Weapon ATK + refine + gear).

StatusMATK:
StatusMATK = 1.5 × INT + floor(DEX / 5) + floor(LUK / 3)

SoftDEF:
SoftDEF = floor(VIT / 2) + floor(AGI / 5) + gear/skills

SoftMDEF:
SoftMDEF = INT + floor(VIT / 5) + floor(DEX / 5) + gear/skills

HIT:
HIT = 175 + BaseLv + DEX + floor(LUK / 3) + gear/skills

FLEE_A and total FLEE:
FLEE_A = 100 + BaseLv + AGI + floor(LUK / 5) + gear/skills
PerfectDodge = floor(LUK / 10) + gear/skills
FLEE = FLEE_A + PerfectDodge

CRIT_rate:
CRIT_rate = 0.3 × LUK + gear/skills

Hit chance vs target:
HitChance = max(5%, min(95%, HIT_attacker − FLEE_defender))

Max HP / Max SP:
MaxHP = ClassBaseHP(BaseLv) × (1 + 0.01 × VIT) + gearHP
MaxSP = ClassBaseSP(BaseLv) × (1 + 0.01 × INT) + gearSP

ASPD (conceptual, with hard cap):
ASPD = ASPD_Base(class, weapon) + f(AGI, DEX)
ASPD ≤ 190
