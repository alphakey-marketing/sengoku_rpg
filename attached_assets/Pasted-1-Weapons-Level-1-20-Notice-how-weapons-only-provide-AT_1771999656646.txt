1. Weapons (Level 1–20)
Notice how weapons only provide ATK. In Classic RO, early weapons rarely gave stat bonuses. Magic weapons (like Rods) did not give flat MATK; instead, they gave a % multiplier to the player's base INT-derived MATK.
​

Daggers (Novice, Thief, Swordman, Mage)

Knife: Required Lv 1 | ATK 17

Cutter: Required Lv 1 | ATK 28

Main Gauche: Required Lv 1 | ATK 43

Swords (Swordman, Merchant, Thief)

Sword: Required Lv 2 | ATK 25

Falchion: Required Lv 2 | ATK 39

Blade: Required Lv 2 | ATK 53

Bows (Archer, Rogue)

Bow: Required Lv 1 | ATK 15

Composite Bow: Required Lv 1 | ATK 29

Great Bow: Required Lv 10 | ATK 43

Magic Staffs (Mage, Acolyte)

Rod: Required Lv 1 | ATK 15 | Special: MATK +15%

Wand: Required Lv 1 | ATK 34 | Special: MATK +15%, INT +1

2. Shields (Off-hand)
Shields gave flat % reduction (DEF) and were usually heavy.

Guard: Required Lv 1 | DEF 3

Buckler: Required Lv 14 | DEF 4

3. Body Armor
Classic RO progressed body armor in +1 DEF increments. Because 1 DEF = 1% less damage, going from a Cotton Shirt to a Coat was a noticeable 4% damage reduction upgrade.

Cotton Shirt: Required Lv 1 | DEF 1

Jacket: Required Lv 1 | DEF 2

Adventurer's Suit: Required Lv 1 | DEF 3

Mantle: Required Lv 1 | DEF 4

Coat: Required Lv 14 | DEF 5

Padded Armor: Required Lv 14 | DEF 6

4. Garments (Cloaks)
Garments offered very little early on without cards.

Hood: Required Lv 1 | DEF 1

Muffler: Required Lv 14 | DEF 2

5. Footgear (Shoes)
Like garments, boots offered tiny defenses early on.

Sandals: Required Lv 1 | DEF 1

Shoes: Required Lv 14 | DEF 2

6. Headgears (Upper / Middle / Lower)
Headgears were where Classic RO added flavor and tiny stat bumps.

Upper Head:

Bandana: Required Lv 1 | DEF 1

Cap: Required Lv 14 | DEF 3

Ribbon: Required Lv 1 | DEF 1 | Special: INT +1, MDEF +3

Middle Head:

Sunglasses: Required Lv 1 | DEF 0 | Special: 5% resistance to Blind status

Lower Head:

Flu Mask: Required Lv 1 | DEF 0 | Special: 10% resistance to Silence status

7. Accessories
Accessories were considered luxury items in early Classic RO. Most players didn't have any until level 20+ unless they were rich enough to afford a "Clip".

Novice Armlet: Required Lv 1 | Special: Max HP +10, Max SP +10

Clip: Required Lv 1 | Special: Max SP +10 (Highly valued because it always had a card slot)

Rosary: Required Lv 20 | Special: LUK +2, MDEF +5

Ring: Required Lv 20 | Special: STR +2

Brooch: Required Lv 20 | Special: AGI +2

Game Design Takeaways for Your Game:
Keep early armor progression flat and simple: Notice how armor goes 1 -> 2 -> 3 -> 4 -> 5 -> 6. You do not need to invent complex armor formulas for early zones. A +1 DEF upgrade should feel rewarding on its own.

Weapons carry the early game: Weapons jump in ATK significantly (Knife 17 -> Main Gauche 43). This makes weapon upgrades the primary dopamine hit for early-game players.

Restrict stats to specific slots: Notice how raw stats (STR, INT, LUK) only appear on headgears and level 20 accessories. Early body armor, shoes, and cloaks only provide DEF. This makes accessories and hats highly desirable for min-maxing.
